Florian Ederer

Allen & Kelli Questrom Professor in Markets, Public Policy and Law
Boston University Questrom School of Business

I am an NBER research associate, a CEPR fellow, a member of the Steering Committee for CEPR's Competition Policy RPN, and an ECGI research member.

My research focuses on what drives and hinders innovation, both in and outside organizations, with a particular emphasis on antitrust.

Curriculum Vitae Google Scholar Twitter / X LinkedIn Email

My work has been published in leading journals and has won numerous awards. It has also informed and influenced policy at the White House, the Federal Trade Commission, the EU Competition Commission, the UK Competition and Markets Authority, and numerous other competition authorities around the world. I was recognized as one of Poets & Quants Best 40 Under 40 business school professors in the world in 2017.
